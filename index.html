How We Work

Many approaches. One principle: use what fits.

Unreal Engine

Real-time 3D environments on LED — full control over lighting, weather, and camera angle.

Plates

Filmed real locations, played back on LED or used in post. The simplest route to a believable world.

Scans

3D captures of real locations that can be relit and viewed from any angle.

Augmented Plates

Real footage enhanced, extended, or transformed — using CG, compositing, or AI tools.
