VP In-House

Building VP capability in-house — without overcommitting.

We help organisations explore whether VP fits their content pipeline — without assuming it means a massive spend or a permanent stage. Feasibility, training, scalable design, and ongoing support.

What It Doesn't Have to Mean

  • Buying a wall before you know what it's for
  • Replacing existing workflows and teams
  • Locking into one vendor's way of working

How We Help

  • Feasibility & planning — does VP make sense for your organisation, and at what scale?
  • Training & upskilling — getting your team confident with the tools and workflows
  • Scalable system design — infrastructure that grows with your needs
  • Ongoing support & documentation — maintenance plans, troubleshooting, and someone to call

Tools We've Built

Tools built by Supervision and actively evolving with real studio use — designed to extend what your VP studio can do without adding complexity.

SuperWiki

A documentation and knowledge system for your VP studio. We capture your entire setup — equipment, infrastructure, workflows — and build an AI-augmented knowledge base around it. Your team gets a browsable wiki and a chat interface that knows exactly how your studio works. When something breaks at 9pm on a Friday, the answer is already there.

SuperSpaces

A modular environment builder for teams who need new environments without going back to an artist every time. We build a library of pre-approved modules — sets, props, lighting setups — that your team can assemble through a simplified interface. Every combination stays within brand guidelines, but gives you far more flexibility than a single fixed environment.

Thinking about VP capability in-house? We'll tell you what makes sense at your scale — and what doesn't.
